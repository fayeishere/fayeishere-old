About Me

Faye Purdum - a developing developer

It's with little surprise that the daughter of an artist and a police officer focuses her time creating worlds and interactions that question process, politics and sustainability. Faye comes to Portland Code School with a BA in Community Development and a keen interest in learning how to leverage mobile technologies to connect and inspire a world of people. When she's not coding for change, you can find her producing an audio drama and animation series.
